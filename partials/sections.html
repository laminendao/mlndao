About Me

My journey and passion as a researcher in Explainable AI and Data Science

Professional Summary

I am a PhD researcher in Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) and Statistical Learning, with a strong passion for making complex models understandable, trustworthy, and useful in real-world applications.

My work focuses on model explainability, dimensionality reduction, time series analysis (especially high-dimensional real-world data), representation learning, and time-aware models.

Curious and impact-driven, I enjoy diving into complex datasets, designing interpretable models, and creating human-comprehensible explanations.
